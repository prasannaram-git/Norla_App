## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Norla uses advanced AI to analyze your nutritional health through simple photos.

🔬 HOW IT WORKS
Take 4 quick photos — your face, eyes, left hand, and right hand. Our AI analyzes 36+ visual biomarkers including skin tone, nail beds, eye sclera, and more to provide detailed nutrition insights.

📊 WHAT YOU GET
• Overall nutrition balance score (0-100)
• Individual scores for 11 key nutrients: Iron, Vitamin B12, Vitamin D, Vitamin A, Folate, Zinc, Protein, Hydration, Vitamin C, Omega-3, and Diet Quality
• Evidence-based explanations for each score
• Personalized dietary recommendations
• Progress tracking with daily score trends

🧠 AI-POWERED ANALYSIS
Norla's clinical-grade AI examines multiple visual indicators:
• Facial complexion and skin health
• Eye sclera coloration
• Nail bed condition and color
• Palm and hand skin texture
• Cross-correlation of findings across all images

📈 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
• Daily scan history with score trends
• Visual progress charts
• Compare scores over time

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• Photos are processed in real-time and never stored
• All data encrypted in transit
• Delete your account and data anytime

⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
Norla is NOT a medical device. All scores are AI-generated predictions for wellness awareness only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice and diagnoses.
