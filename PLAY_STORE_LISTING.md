## FULL DESCRIPTION (for Play Store — copy exactly)

```
Norla uses advanced AI to analyze visual biomarkers in your photos and generate personalized nutrition support scores.

📸 HOW IT WORKS
1. Take 3 simple photos — your face, one eye, and your hand
2. Answer 18 quick lifestyle questions about your diet and habits
3. Get a detailed nutrition analysis in under 60 seconds

📊 WHAT YOU GET
• 11 nutrient support scores (Iron, Vitamin B12, D, A, Folate, Zinc, Protein, Hydration, Vitamin C, Omega-3, and Diet Quality)
• Overall wellness balance score (0-100)
• Priority focus areas — know exactly where to improve
• Personalized food and lifestyle recommendations
• Scan history to track changes over time

🧠 POWERED BY AI
Norla uses Google Gemini Vision AI to analyze visual patterns in your photos — nail condition, skin tone, eye clarity, and more. Combined with your lifestyle questionnaire, it generates a comprehensive nutrition profile.

⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
Norla is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide clinical diagnoses or lab measurements. Results are AI-generated wellness predictions for awareness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

🔒 YOUR PRIVACY
• Photos are processed for analysis only and not permanently stored
• Your data is encrypted and securely stored
• We never sell your data to third parties

📱 FEATURES
• Works offline for viewing past results
• Clean, intuitive mobile interface
• Secure phone number verification via WhatsApp
• Scan history with progress tracking
```
